Let's Play Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Each crystal has a different point value. You win the game by acquiring enough points to equal to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

You can discover each crystal's value by clicking on it.

Each time the game starts over, the value of the crystals will change.

Good luck collecting the right crystals! :)
